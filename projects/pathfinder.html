How it Works

  • The Mission: Find the fastest route from Green to Blue.
  • Roadblocks: Draw Red walls to create a maze that the system must solve.
  • The Search: The "Scout" (light yellow) explores the map to find every available opening.
  • Navigation: The computer calculates the "cost" of every move to ensure it never takes a longer turn than necessary.
  • Success: The solid yellow line is the final, most efficient path discovered.
